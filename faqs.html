  • Is this for real?

    Yes, it is! It's as exciting for us to bring the magic of cinema to real people. What we do is allow you to relive the biggest moments of your life in the way that they deserve to be remembered. With respect and perspective.

  • How much do your films cost?

    Most of the budgeting variables depend upon the size and type of the wedding. Each wedding film is customisable starting from INR 10L per day (approx. $10000). We take into consideration a lot of factors including your story, personality and how much of the wedding you want to remember. The cost also depends upon the size of crew and is inclusive of other talent. Added services such as a personalised soundtrack, 360 videos, aerial videography, on-site edits and any other special requirements would be an additional cost. Having said that, each wedding is unique and your films could cost a little less, or much more. Our approach to film-making and budgeting isn't quite as cut dry as a 'package' or 'fixed price'. So whether you're having a Bollywood style grand production or an intimate backyard wedding, we'd like to treat it with the poignancy and grandeur it deserves. Write to us and we'll do our best to make it work for you.
